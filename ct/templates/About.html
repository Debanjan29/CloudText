Welcome to CloudText

CloudText is a web app that allows you to share codes and texts without the need for logging in or signing up. It's simple:

  1. Paste your text in the textarea below.
  2. Click the "Upload" button to get a 4-character code.
  3. Store this code safely.

To retrieve your data:

  1. Enter the code in the search box.
  2. Your data will be displayed.

Remember: Your code is not Case-Sensitive!!.

Data Retention: All shared data will be securely stored up until the 24th of every month.
